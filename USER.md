# Board Game Community Hub – User Guide

Welcome to the Board Game Community Hub! This app lets you track games and your scores in a simple, fun way.

## 1. Getting Started

Before you can use the app, you’ll need to create an account:

1. Click **Sign Up**.
2. Enter your **name, email, and password**.
3. Submit the form to create your account.

If you already have an account, click **Log In** and enter your credentials.

Once logged in, you can access games and your scores.

---

## 2. User Dashboard

After logging in, you’ll see your dashboard:

- **Your Scores** – View all scores you’ve submitted for different games.
- **Games You Play** – See a list of all games you have participated in.
- **Highest Score Feature** – Click on your highest score in a game to view all other scores you’ve submitted across other games.

---

## 3. Managing Games

Games are the backbone of the hub. You can:

### Add a Game

1. Go to the **Games** section.
2. Click **New Game**.
3. Enter the **game name** and **genre**.
4. Submit to save the game.

### View Game Details

1. Click a game to see all scores submitted by users.
2. Click a **user’s highest score** to view all of that user’s scores in other games.

---

## 4. Submitting Scores

1. Click **Submit Score** for a specific game.
2. Enter **points scored** and optionally a **round number or date**.
3. Submit the form to update your profile and leaderboard.

---

## 5. Authentication

- You must be **logged in** to submit scores or add games.
- Use **Log Out** when finished to secure your account.

---

## 6. Validation Rules

- All fields are required unless stated otherwise.
- Unique constraints prevent duplicate user emails.
- Scores must be numeric.
- Validation errors appear inline in the forms if there’s an issue.

---

## 7. Navigation Tips

- Use the **top menu** to switch between Dashboard and Games.
- Forms are straightforward and will show errors if something is missing.
